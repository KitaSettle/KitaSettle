# Executive Brief Engine v1.0

## Purpose

The Executive Brief helps the founder know exactly what deserves attention today.

## Inputs

Future inputs:
- Calendar
- Email
- Tasks
- Reports
- Proposals
- Business documents
- Financial data
- Notes
- Previous decisions

Alpha input:
- Mock data

## Output Sections

1. Morning summary
2. Top 3 priorities
3. Decisions waiting
4. Risks
5. Opportunities
6. AI-prepared work
7. Recommended focus
8. Founder workload estimate

## Example

Good morning, Dan.

Your Executive Brief is ready.

Today’s recommended focus:
Finalise the steelworks proposal and prepare tomorrow’s aviation training session.

Top priorities:
1. Review steelworks proposal
2. Prepare CBTA lesson
3. Capture one business idea for review

Decisions waiting:
- Approve proposal direction
- Confirm pricing assumptions

Risks:
- Proposal lacks project photos
- Training slides need stronger scenario discussion

Opportunities:
- Turn aviation lesson framework into reusable product module
